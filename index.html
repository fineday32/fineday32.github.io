Welcome! I am currently pursuing graduate study to refine my knowledge and skills. I am drawn to it by the combination of my interest and real-world technical trend it boasts, and finally expect to make a strong contribution to the world.

I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering with a double degree in Economics from National Taiwan University in 2014. And my interest in the patterns of human thoughts and interaction also motivated me to join the Mobile and HCI Research Laboratory, directed by Prof. Mike Y. Chen.

My research is focused on Human-Computer Interaction, such as realizing the changes in behavior patterns with technology, usage of wearable devices as an integral part of daily life, and the change of connection between people through technology. More detailed information can be found in my Projects.
